Hotlines

Crisis Text Line: Text “HELLO” to 741741

Veterans Crisis Line: Call 1-800-273-TALK (8255) and press 1 or text to 838255

Disaster Distress Helpline: Call 1-800-985-5990 or text “TalkWithUs” to 66746

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA): Call the SAMHSA Treatment Referral Helpline at 1-800-662-HELP (4357)
