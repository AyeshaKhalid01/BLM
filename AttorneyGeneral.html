Attorney General Directory

The Attorney General of each of the 50 states is essentially the chief legal advisor to the state government and the people’s lawyer of the state. The Attorney General’s role is to act on behalf of a state’s people and to act in the state’s best interest to protect its law of order, organizations, resources.
